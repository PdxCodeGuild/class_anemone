Common Frog: Rana temporaria

SUMMARY

Rana Temporaria mostly land dwelling animal, originally from Europe but can be found around the globe to include Japan and Americas. These froggos are typically just under an ounce in weight measuring about 2 1/2" in length and can live past 10 years.

Quote: “If it’s your job to eat a frog, it’s best to do it first thing in the morning. And if it’s your job to eat two frogs, it’s best to eat the biggest one first.” - Mark Twain.

Places the Common Frog calls home...

  • Japan
  • Ireland
  • USA
  • Russia
  • etc.
