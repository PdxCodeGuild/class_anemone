Harry Potter

Harry was known as "the boy who lived" as he escaped his death from the darkest wizard of all time. He is a fictional character and the titular protagonist in J. K. Rowling 's series of eponymous novels. The majority of the books' plot covers seven years in the life of the orphan Harry, who, on his eleventh birthday, learns he is a wizard.
