Archer is an adult animated sitcom with a James Bond style action based on a
secret agent named Sterling Malory Archer. Archer, a fictional character is an extremely narcissistic,
arrogant, selfish, insensitive, self-absorbed, and sex-crazed man who is constantly focused on
himself and his own needs and desires. He arrogantly believes himself to understand any
situation better than his colleagues. Thus, Archer selfishly refuses to admit when he is proven wrong
and will go to extreme lengths to cover up his mistakes, particularly when they may incur his mother's
scorn. While working as a secret agent for the International Secret Intelligence Service,
also known as ISIS, he aims to be the center of attention while trying to defreat his long time
rival Barry Dylan, a former KGB sypmaster.

Places visited by the International Secret Intelligence Service:

  • Tangier, Morocco
  • Gstaad, Switzerland
  • St Tropez, France
