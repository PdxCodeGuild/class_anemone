Jimi Hendrix

When the power of love overcomes the love of power, the world will know peace.




Jimi Hendrix is considered by many to be the greatest rock guitarist ever. After years of playing on the R&B circuit with a number of famous artists, Jimmy Hendrix became Jimi Hendrix and formed the Jimi Hendrix Experience in 1966. One of his most iconic performances was as the closing act at Woodstock, where he played a transcendent version of the Star Spangled Banner. He was born on November 27, 1962 in Seattle. He died on September 18, 1970.
